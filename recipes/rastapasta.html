Rasta Pasta


Description

Rasta pasta is a creamy pasta tossed with Caribbean style jerk chicken and sauteeed bell peppers. This dish is so full of flavor, and makes a great weeknight dinner that can be made in just half an hour! It's creamy, rich, spicy, and cheesy.

Ingredients Required

  • Rotisserie Chicken
  • Bell Peppers
  • Rotini Pasta
  • Green Onions
  • Jerk Seasoning
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Chicken Stock
